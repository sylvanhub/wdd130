Brief History of the Pearl's Touch Unisex Salon

The salon was founded in 2016 and the initial aim was to make hair for the students in the federal college of agriculture and the college of animal health and production technology. The salon specialises in barbing, dreadlock, Ghana weaving and braiding for woman, nails and eye lashes fixing, hair treatment and dread nursing, hair colouring, and training programs for students who want to learn the skill at an affordable rate. This salon was saddled with the responsibility of offering the best service at an affordable rate to the students and their lecturers. Pearl's touch unisex salon website is built primarily for the sake of our customers.
